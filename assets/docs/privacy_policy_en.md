# Privacy Policy

At **ASMR Battle Factory**, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This application is designed to function locally with full respect for your data.

## 1. Data Collection
We **do not collect** any personal data, images, or identifiable information. The app operates independently on your device.

### API Keys
- The app requires a Gemini API Key (Google) to function.
- This key is stored **locally only** and encrypted using Secure Storage.
- We do not share this key with our servers or any third parties.

## 2. Local Storage
We use **Hive** technology to store:
- App settings (Language, Theme).
- History of generated battles.
- Onboarding status.

You can clear this data at any time via: **Settings > Clear Cache**.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties. The only internet connection is directly between your device and Google servers (Gemini API) for text generation.

## 4. Generated Content
All generated scripts and scenarios belong to you and are stored on your device.
